A REPOSITORY OF PLANT AND ITS SPECIES

Plants are employed in different medicinal systems and ethnic medicines. India has rich tradition of herbal medicines and it has outstanding contributions not only in form of Ayurveda & Siddha but also in the discovery of modern drugs and pharmacological research. Our medicinal plant database provides information on scientific name, family, vernacular name, medicinal uses, location of species and images of herbarium specimen. In first phase, a total of 100 species are enlisted and about 200 species will be added in next phase.
