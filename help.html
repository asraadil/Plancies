A repository of plants & its species , as the term itself indicates that it is a repository of different type of medicinal plants, their scientific name , their family and their uses. Plants synthesize hundreds of chemical compounds for various functions, including defense and protection against insects, fungi, diseases, and herbivorous mammals.Medicinal plants are widely used in non-industrialized societies, mainly because they are readily available and cheaper than modern medicines.
In modern medicine, around a quarter of the drugs prescribed to patients are derived from medicinal plants, and they are rigorously tested.In other systems of medicine, medicinal plants may constitute the majority of what are often informal attempted treatments, not tested scientifically.

    Steps to get the details of medicinal plants species availabe in this plant repository


  • Click on browse tab
  • Scroll down to the search bar
  • In search you can enter species name,family or common name
  • If the rows appear after the search click on the last column to view the details
  • If nothing appears after your search then their might be spelling mistake or that particular species is not present in this repository
  • You can also generate pdf of the species details.
